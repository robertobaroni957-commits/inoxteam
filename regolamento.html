1. Masters Winter Tour

  • Individual competition with rankings for: Points, Time, Sprint and Climb.
  • Start grids reported in the Racepass.
  • General rules: use of prohibited techniques or equipment is forbidden; first violation results in disqualification (DQ).

Specific race rules

  • Free choice of bike and wheels (TT bikes allowed only in time trial events).
  • No power-ups.
  • ERG mode is forbidden.

Time Trial (iTT) rules

  • Drafting disabled.
  • Free choice of bike and wheels (TT bike recommended).
  • No power-ups.
  • ERG mode is forbidden.

2. Riders

  • Active ZwiftPower account required.
  • Smart or interactive trainer required. If no data is transmitted, points will be voided.
  • Heart-rate monitor mandatory; malfunctions will be handled by the organisers.
  • Correct height and weight in Zwift profile; significant discrepancies may lead to disqualification.

4. Rankings & Points

Individual points ranking

  • Points accumulated from iTT and Points stages.
  • Total points = FIN + FTS + FAL.
  • Each stage counts toward the final ranking.
  • Tie: FIN points are used to break ties.

Sprint Specialist Ranking

  • FTS and FAL sprint points from iTT and Points stages.
  • Tie: iTT points are used to break ties.

Climber Specialist (KOM) Ranking

  • FTS and FAL KOM points from iTT and Points stages.
  • Tie: iTT points are used to break ties.

Individual Time Ranking

  • Sum of times from each stage, including FIN, FAL and FTS bonuses.
  • Non participation or late entry: time of the last classified rider.

5. Points in Different Races

Points races (Points & Luna Park)

  • FIN: see table.

  • Punti totali = FIN + FTS + FAL.

iTT Climber races

  • FIN: see table.
  • FTS e FAL non assegnati.
  • Punti totali = FIN.

iTT stages

  • FIN: see table.
  • FTS: top 10 Sprint/KOM.
  • FAL: non awarded.
  • Punti totali = FIN + FTS.
